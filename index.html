About Us

Opinac is a small company of four people based in Miami, FL.

It initially focused on selling clothing and other items featuring custom designs, allowing us to explore what it was like to have a business and create personally-designed products. Nowadays, we are in the process of transitioning into an indie game studio. What initially began as an experiment in merchandise has started to evolve into a small but dedicated team, where we are now starting to work on creating games that bring our visions to life.

The name "Opinac" doesn't have a specific meaning. Over time, though, it has come to represent our creativity, whether through design or interactive storytelling.

Right now, we are currently in the process of creating our first major project. More details will be revealed at a later date.
